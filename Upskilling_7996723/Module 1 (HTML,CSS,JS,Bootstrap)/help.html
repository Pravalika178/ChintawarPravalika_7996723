Community Event Portal Help Center

Getting Started

Welcome to the Local Community Event Portal. This platform helps residents discover events, register online, provide feedback, and find nearby activities.

How to Register for an Event

  • Open the Event Registration section.
  • Enter your name and email address.
  • Select an event date and event type.
  • Provide additional information if required.
  • Click Submit Registration.

Finding Nearby Events

Click the Find Nearby Events button and allow location access. The portal will display your coordinates and help locate events near you.

Saving Preferences

Your preferred event type can be saved locally in your browser. The next time you visit, your previous selection will automatically appear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why am I not receiving confirmation?

Ensure you entered a valid email address and completed all required fields.

Q: Why is my location not displayed?

Check whether location permission has been granted in your browser settings.

Q: Can I change my event preference later?

Yes. Select a different event type and save preferences again.

Q: How do I clear saved preferences?

Use the Clear Preferences button available on the portal homepage.

Browser Support

This portal is optimized for modern browsers such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, and Safari.
